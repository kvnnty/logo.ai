User Guide

Complete User Guide: Learn how to use LogoAIpro v1.0, from creating your first AI-generated logo to managing your logo history and understanding the credit system.

Getting Started

1. Account Creation

  1. Sign Up: Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up" on the homepage
  2. Choose Method: Sign up with email/password or Google OAuth
  3. Verify Email: Check your email and verify your account (if required)
  4. Dashboard Access: Automatically redirected to your dashboard after registration
  5. Free Credits: Receive 10 free credits to test the platform

2. Understanding Credits

Credit System:
  • Free Credits: All new users receive 10 free credits
  • Credit Packages: Purchase credits in packages (Basic: 50, Pro: 150, Enterprise: 500 credits)
  • Logo Cost: Each logo generation costs 1 credit
  • Buy More: Purchase credits from Dashboard ‚Üí Credits
  • No Expiration: Credits never expire

Creating Your Logo

LogoAIpro uses a simple 6-step wizard to create professional logos. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Brand Name

  1. Navigate: Go to /generate or click "Generate Logo" from the dashboard
  2. Enter Name: Type your company or brand name in the input field
  3. Continue: Click "Next" to proceed to style selection
Tip: Use your actual brand name as it will appear in the generated logo. Keep it concise for best results.

Step 2: Style Selection

Choose from 6 professional design styles:

  • Minimal: Simple, timeless, single-color with negative space and flat design
  • Technology: Detailed, sharp, cinematic, minimalist with clean lines
  • Corporate: Modern, forward-thinking, geometric shapes with professional appeal
  • Creative: Playful, bright bold colors, rounded shapes, lively designs
  • Abstract: Artistic, unique shapes, patterns, and visually interesting textures
  • Flashy: Attention-grabbing, bold, futuristic with vibrant neon colors
  1. Browse Styles: Review all 6 style options
  2. Select Style: Click on your preferred style
  3. Continue: Click "Next" to proceed

Step 3: Colors & AI Model

Customize your logo's appearance and choose the AI model:

  1. Primary Color: Select the main color for your logo (Blue, Red, Orange, Green, Purple, Black)
  2. Background Color: Choose the background color (White, Light Gray, Light Red, Black, Light Blue, Light Green)
  3. AI Model: Choose between:
    • Flux Schnell: Fast generation, optimized for realistic logos
    • Flux Dev: Advanced model for enhanced realism and complex designs
  4. Continue: Click "Next" to proceed

Step 4: Size & Quality

Select the output dimensions and quality:

  1. Image Size: Choose from:
    • Small (256x256): For web icons and small applications
    • Medium (512x512): Standard size for most use cases
    • Large (1024x1024): High resolution for print and large displays
  2. Quality: Select quality level:
    • Standard: Good quality, faster generation
    • HD: Higher quality, slightly longer generation time
  3. Continue: Click "Next" to proceed

Step 5: Additional Details

Provide optional information about your brand:

  1. Brand Personality: Describe your brand's personality, target audience, or specific preferences
  2. Examples: "Modern tech startup targeting millennials" or "Eco-friendly brand with natural themes"
  3. Optional: This step is optional - you can skip it if you don't have specific requirements
  4. Generate: Click "Generate Logo" to create your logo

Step 6: Generated Logo

Once generation is complete, you can:

  1. View Logo: See your generated logo in the preview area
  2. Download: Click "Download" to save your logo as a WebP file
  3. Generate New: Click "Generate New" to create another variation
  4. View History: Your logo is automatically saved to your history
Success! Your logo is now saved to your account and can be accessed from the History page.

Tips & Best Practices

Getting Better Results

  • Clear Brand Name: Use your actual brand name for best results
  • Style Selection: Choose a style that matches your brand identity
  • Color Choice: Select colors that represent your brand
  • Additional Details: Provide specific information about your brand for more tailored results

Saving Credits

  • Preview First: Review all options before generating
  • Save Results: Download logos you like immediately
  • Buy Larger Packs: Larger packs offer better value per credit
  • Use Free Credits: Test the platform with your 10 free credits first

Rate Limiting

Rate Limit: LogoAIpro has a rate limit of 10 logo generations per 30 days per user. This helps prevent abuse and ensures fair usage for all users.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • No Credits: Purchase credits from the Credits page
  • Generation Failed: Check your internet connection and try again
  • Rate Limited: Wait for the rate limit period to reset (30 days)
  • Download Issues: Try a different browser or check browser permissions
